About Project

A poultry farm is an agricultural unit where birds such as chickens are raised for the production of eggs and meat. It is an important part of the livestock sector and contributes to food supply as well as employment generation in rural and urban areas. Proper poultry farming requires scientific management of feeding, housing, sanitation, and disease control to maintain healthy birds and ensure good productivity. Modern poultry farms use improved equipment for feeding, watering, ventilation, and egg collection. Poultry farming is considered a profitable business because of its fast production cycle and growing market demand.
