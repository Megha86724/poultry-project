Feeding and Nutrition - Poultry Farming

Introduction

Feed and nutrition are very important in poultry farming. Proper nutrition helps in growth, egg production, and disease prevention. A balanced diet includes proteins, carbohydrates, fats, vitamins, minerals, and clean water.

Nutritional Table

Nutrient Function Sources
Proteins Growth and repair Soybean, fish meal
Carbohydrates Energy source Maize, wheat
Fats Extra energy Oil seeds
Vitamins Maintain health Green vegetables
Minerals Bone & eggshell strength Calcium, phosphorus
Water Essential for digestion Clean water

Conclusion

Balanced feed ensures healthy poultry and better production. Proper nutrition management is key to successful poultry farming.
